Instructions: How to Find a Buddy

1. Make sure you have your classes saved with your profile. Go to Settings â†’ Edit Classes and select your classes from the drop down menu and click Save.

2. Go to the Find Buddies tab and select your class from the dropdown menu. If there are other students in the class with profiles, their names should pop up. From here, you can either view their profile or start a chat.

3. Find existing chats under Chats in the navbar. Or, try visiting Class Forums to have a group conversation with everyone in a certain class.

4. After finding a time to study with your new buddy, you can create schedule a study session with them by visiting Study Sessions. Or, try joining an existing session for one of your classes using the search function. All of your past and upcoming study sessions will be displayed here on your dashboard.
